Types of Disease Prevention

General Disease Prevention

  • Wash hands regularly with soap and water.
  • Maintain proper hygiene and clean environment.
  • Use clean drinking water and eat well-cooked food.
  • Get vaccinated when necessary.

Cholera

  • Drink only safe and treated water.
  • Wash hands with soap before eating and after using the toilet.
  • Avoid raw or undercooked seafood and street food.
  • Use latrines and ensure proper sewage disposal.

Poor Sanitation & Waste Disposal

  • Dispose of waste in designated areas or bins.
  • Do not dump waste near water sources.
  • Educate the community on safe sanitation practices.
  • Use toilets and maintain clean facilities.

Typhoid Fever

  • Boil or treat drinking water.
  • Wash fruits and vegetables before eating.
  • Get vaccinated if in high-risk area.
  • Practice proper hand hygiene consistently.
