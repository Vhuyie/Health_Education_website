Common Injuries &
Their Treatment

  • Clean wounds with clean water and cover with sterile dressing.
  • Use bandages to secure dressings and control bleeding.
  • Splint broken bones to prevent movement.
  • Seek medical attention if injury is severe or worsening.

Burns & Heat-Related Illnesses

  • Cool burns immediately with running water (at least 10 min).
  • Do not apply ice or greasy substances.
  • For heat stroke, move to shade and cool down.
  • Seek emergency help for severe burns or heat stroke.
